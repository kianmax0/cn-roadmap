Intended audience: undergraduates, graduate students, and self-learners transitioning into computational neuroscience from CS/AI, math/physics, biology/medicine, or psychology/cognitive science  
Default pace: 8–12 hours/week for about 64 weeks; a 24-week fast track and an 18–24 month deep-dive version are also provided at the end  
Note: the field is generally called **computational neuroscience** in English.
